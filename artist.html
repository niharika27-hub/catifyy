A. R. Rahman

The “Mozart of Madras,” is a world-renowned composer, singer, and music producer whose soulful melodies and innovative soundscapes have touched millions across the globe.Winner of two Academy Awards, Grammy Awards, and countless honors, Rahman blends Indian classical music with global influences, creating timeless pieces that inspire and connect people everywhere.

Sonu Nigam

Sonu Nigam – One of India’s most versatile and celebrated playback singers, Sonu Nigam’s golden voice has defined an era of Bollywood music.From soulful ballads to high-energy hits, his unmatched range and emotion have made him a global icon.



Lata Mangeshkar

Lata Mangeshkar – Revered as the “Nightingale of India,” Lata Mangeshkar’s legendary voice defined Indian music for over seven decades. With thousands of timeless songs across multiple languages, her melodies remain etched in the hearts of millions.A Bharat Ratna awardee, she is celebrated as one of the greatest and most influential singers in the history of Indian music.



Asha Bhosle

Asha Bhosle – Known for her versatility and vibrant voice, Asha Bhosle has sung in thousands of films and in over 20 languages. From soulful ghazals to peppy Bollywood hits, her music spans every mood and genre. A Guinness World Record holder for the most recorded artist in music history, she remains an everlasting icon of Indian playback singing.
